Who we are

Our Journey

Nkukwa Communication Ltd is a registered and licensed by government of Uganda as a 360-degree storytelling, documentation, graphic designing, branding, and marketing communication agency in Uganda with a reputation for delivering market-based communication solutions to individual clients, government, the Private sector and NGO. With over 30 years of combined experience, Nkukwa Communications staff provide complete integrated communications solutions for clients from various sector segments in Uganda. We believe that visual communication is the future of marketing and communications worldover. We have invested in developing a professional team with hands-on, cutting-edge expertise in storytelling, visual documentation and can proudly describe ourselves as “the visual communication gurus”.
